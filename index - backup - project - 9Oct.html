    About Us

    A little bit about us and how we can help you...

    Established in 1990, our reputation has been built on reliability, excellent service, loyalty to our customers and continuous development of new, innovative textiles. Our textiles are well known for their quality, durability, and continuity at competitive prices.

    We offer a wide variety of specially imported textiles suitable for the manufacture of products for various industries. Our innovative collection is available in a wide range of colours and includes many products that are suitable for dye sublimation "heat transfer printing".

    Custom textiles, colours or design may also be specially ordered through us on a confirmed order basis. Our buyers can source any special requirements, quickly and efficiently.
